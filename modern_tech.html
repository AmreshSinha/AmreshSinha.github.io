Farming Modern Days Technologies

The life line of all farmers, Tractors. It helps in ploughing, harvesting, digging and much more. It acts as a best friend to a farmer. It's a must for every farmer. Some of the major companies of tractor are: John Deere, Ashok Leyland, Mahindra, etc.

Farming Equipments :

  • Cultipacker - Cultipackers are pulled behind tractors to firm seedbeds before seeding to set up your planting for good seed-to-soil contact.
